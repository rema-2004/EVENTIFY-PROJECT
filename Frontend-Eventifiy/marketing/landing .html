Everything you need to know about Eventify.

Eventify is an AI-powered platform that connects students and graduates with hackathons, competitions, internships, courses and conferences. Our AI assistant Rafeeq matches opportunities to your skills, interests and career goals — so you never miss the right one.
Yes, Eventify is completely free for students. You can create a profile, explore opportunities, apply with one click, and use Rafeeq AI — all at no cost. Organizations pay a small fee to post opportunities and access our talent pool.
Eventify is open to university students, fresh graduates and early-career professionals across all disciplines. Whether you're in computer science, business, design or any other field — if you're looking for opportunities, Eventify is for you.
When you create your profile, Rafeeq AI analyzes your skills, interests, academic background and career goals. It then scans thousands of opportunities and ranks them by relevance — giving you a personalized match score for each one. The more you interact, the smarter it gets.
Absolutely. Companies, universities and communities can register as organizations on Eventify. They can post hackathons, internships, competitions and courses — then use our AI matching to find the most qualified applicants automatically.
